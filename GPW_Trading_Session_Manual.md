## Detailed Trading Phases

### Standard Continuous Trading Sequence
1. **Opening Call** (Pre-opening): 08:55 - 09:00 (5 minutes)
2. **Trading Session**: 09:00 - 16:50 (Main trading)
3. **Closing Call**: 16:50 - 16:50 (Immediate)
4. **Trading at Last**: 16:50 - 16:50 (For specific instruments)

### Derivatives Detailed Phases (Stock Futures Example)
1. **Opening Call**: 08:30 - 08:45 (15 minutes)
2. **Market Opening**: 08:45
3. **Trading Session**: 08:45 - 16:50 (7 hours 5 minutes)
4. **Closing Call**: 16:50 - 17:00 (10 minutes)
5. **Closing**: 17:00
6. **Trading at Last**: 17:00 - 17:05 (5 minutes)

## Market Phases Explained

| Phase | Description | Purpose |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Opening Call** | Pre-opening order collection | Price discovery, order balancing |
| **Trading Session** | Continuous order matching | Main trading activity |
| **Closing Call** | Final order collection | Closing price determination |
| **Trading at Last** | Trade only at last price | Final execution opportunity |
| **End of Session** | Market closure | Trading suspended |
| **Change of Timetable** | Special schedule modifications | Exceptional circumstances |

## Trading Modes

### Continuous Trading
- **Description:** Standard order-driven trading with real-time execution
- **Availability:** All major market segments  
- **Order Types:** Market, Limit, Stop, Stop-Limit
- **Features:** Continuous order matching, price transparency

### Single Price Auction
- **Description:** Call auction with single price determination
- **Subtypes:** 
  - Double Auction: Multiple price discovery sessions
  - Single Auction (1 fix): Once-daily price setting
- **Usage:** Opening/closing procedures, illiquid securities

## Special Procedures

### Pre-Auction Period
- **Duration:** 5-15 minutes before market opening
- **Purpose:** Order collection without execution
- **Benefit:** Price discovery and liquidity building

### Post-Trading Procedures
- **Closing Auction:** 5-10 minutes for closing price determination
- **Trading at Last:** 5 minutes for final executions at closing price
- **After Hours:** Limited trading for specific derivatives

### Cross-Trading
- Available between different market segments
- Subject to specific regulatory conditions
- Facilitates price discovery and liquidity

## Operational Notes

- **Technical Breaks:** No scheduled breaks during regular trading hours
- **Extended Hours:** Derivatives offer extended pre/post market trading
- **Holiday Schedule:** No trading on Polish national holidays
- **Special Circumstances:** Timetable changes announced via operational communiqués
- **Time Zone:** All times in Central European Time (CET/CEST)
